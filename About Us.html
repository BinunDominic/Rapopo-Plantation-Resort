About Rapopo Plantation Resort

Rapopo Plantation Resort is located in the stunning natural landscapes of Papua New Guinea, offering a serene and peaceful escape for both relaxation and adventure. The resort is set in lush tropical surroundings, with breathtaking views of the ocean, and is designed to blend with the local culture and environment.

Founded in 2000, Rapopo Plantation Resort has grown into a premier destination, known for its warm hospitality, excellent service, and commitment to providing an authentic Pacific experience. Guests can enjoy a variety of accommodations, ranging from luxurious villas to cozy cottages, all designed for comfort and relaxation.

At Rapopo Plantation Resort, you can indulge in exciting activities such as snorkeling, diving, hiking, and cultural excursions. The resort is also home to a wide range of dining options that showcase the best local and international cuisines, all prepared using fresh, locally sourced ingredients.

Whether you're seeking a peaceful retreat or an action-packed getaway, Rapopo Plantation Resort offers the perfect blend of nature, culture, and comfort. Join us for an unforgettable experience!
