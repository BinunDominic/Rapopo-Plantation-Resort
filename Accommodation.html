Accommodation at Rapopo Plantation Resort

At Rapopo Plantation Resort, we offer a variety of luxurious and comfortable accommodations designed to suit all your needs. Our rooms are equipped with modern amenities and offer breathtaking views of the lush tropical surroundings. Whether you're here for a short stay or an extended holiday, we have the perfect room for you.

Garden View Room

Enjoy the tranquility of our Garden View. These rooms feature beautiful views of the lush gardens and are perfect for relaxation. Equipped with air conditioning, free Wi-Fi, and a comfortable bed, your stay will be peaceful and restful.

Ocean View room

p>Our Ocean View Rooms offer stunning vistas of the Pacific Ocean. These rooms are designed for ultimate comfort, featuring spacious interiors, a private balcony, and modern amenities like a mini-fridge and flat-screen TV. Perfect for those who want to wake up to the sound of the ocean waves.

Luxury Villa

For the ultimate resort experience, our Luxury Villas provide a lavish retreat. These villas include a private pool, a spacious living area, a fully equipped kitchen, and a patio with panoramic views of the resort's beautiful landscape. Ideal for families or groups seeking extra space and privacy.
