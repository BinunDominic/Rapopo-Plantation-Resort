Activities at Rapopo Plantation Resort

At Rapopo Plantation Resort, we offer a variety of exciting activities that allow you to explore the beauty of Papua New Guinea, relax, and create unforgettable memories. Whether you're an adventure enthusiast or seeking peaceful relaxation, we have something for everyone. Check out some of the activities we offer below:

Kayaking

Explore the coastline and nearby islands by kayak. This peaceful yet adventurous activity offers a unique way to see the stunning scenery and marine life from a different perspective.

Scuba Diving

For those who are certified divers, our resort offers scuba diving excursions to some of the most breathtaking dive sites in the region, filled with vibrant coral reefs and marine creatures.
